About Us

The Nsawo RCC Medical Centre is a community-driven healthcare initiative dedicated to improving the well-being of the residents in Nsawo Village, Luweero District, Uganda. Our journey began through the joint efforts of local residents, Rotary grants, and international sponsors, aiming to uplift the community by providing comprehensive healthcare services and promoting a healthier lifestyle.

Our Commitment to Health and Community

At Nsawo RCC Medical Centre, we prioritize safe maternity care, pediatric health, and community well-being. We understand the critical importance of addressing maternal health concerns, preventing diseases like malaria, and ensuring children's health for a thriving community.

  • Providing essential medical care services for mothers and children in the Luweero District.
  • Conducting community outreach programs focusing on health education and disease prevention.
  • Collaborating with local stakeholders and international sponsors to enhance healthcare accessibility and quality.

Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals, supported by generous contributions from compassionate individuals like Hugh Toloui and Flor, strives to expand our facilities and services to address evolving healthcare needs. Together, we aim to build a healthier future for Nsawo Village and beyond.

Our Core Values

Workplace Culture

We strive to cultivate a positive workplace atmosphere by fostering a positive attitude among our team members.

Caring and Compassion

We approach our work and interactions with compassion, kindness, and respect for both our patients and colleagues.

Honesty and Integrity

We are committed to transparency, acting with honesty and integrity in all aspects of our services and interactions.

Team Work

We believe in the power of teamwork, understanding that collaboration and collective efforts surpass individual achievements.

Community Engagement

We recognize our role in local communities and strive to understand and meet their healthcare needs.

Excellence and Quality

We are dedicated to delivering excellent and high-quality healthcare services, continuously aiming to exceed our customers' expectations.

We are committed to creating an educational environment for patients, employees, and medical professionals focused on serving disadvantaged communities.
